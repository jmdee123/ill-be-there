Add your Anthropic API key to unlock reflections
The daily and weekly reflections are powered by Claude AI. Add your own key — it syncs securely to your account so it works on every device you log in from.
How to get your key
  1. Go to console.anthropic.com
  2. Sign up or log in, then go to API Keys
  3. Click Create Key, copy it, paste below
Your key is encrypted and stored against your account — it syncs across all your devices automatically.
